About Us

This club was created with the aim of creating activities in which dog owners can participate with their animals and socialize with other dog owners. We organize and accept donations that are used only to maintain the club or to help other animal organizations.

  5. Dogs encourage you to move

  8. Dogs make us more social
  9. Dogs make us happier
